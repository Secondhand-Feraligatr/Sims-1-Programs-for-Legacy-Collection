Overview
--------

Make-A-Celebrity allows you to create your perfect match and visit with them in Studio Town.   
Customize their looks, personality, interests and superstardom!  Did you make an undeniable Superstar that everyone wants to get to know?  Then upload 'em to the  
Celebrity Exchange at thesims.ea.com and see how they stack up against the competition.
You must have The Sims Superstar installed in order to use The Sims Make-A-Celebrity.


Make-A-Celebrity
-----------
Instructions on the use of The Sims Make-A-Celebrity.
-----------------------------------------------
To make a Celebrity follow these simple steps:
1. Click the "Create" button after Make-A-Celebrity loads.
2. Select gender, body type, skin type, body and head.  Note: only skins that can be applied to that body will appear. If there isn't a skin that can be placed on a body, an incorrect skin may appear. The message window will inform you of any errors in your combination. Click "Next".
3. Choose a high-fashion outfit.  This will be the outfit your Celebrity wears when he/she appears in Studio Town. Click "Next".  
4. Enter a name (less than 30 characters) and a description (1,024 characters or less) for your Celebrity.  Click "Next".
5. Give your Celebrity a personality.  You may use up to 25 personality points or click on the "Astrological Sign" box to give your Celebrity a personality based on an astrological sign.  Click "Next".
6. Assign interests to your Celebrity.  You must provide 5 high and 5 low interests.  Click "Next".
7. Choose the fame level for your Celebrity.  They can only be in the fame track (obviously).  Click "Next".
8. You may click "Back" to modify any of your Celebrity's traits or Click "Save Sim" to finish.
9. Enter the name you wish to save the Celebrity as and click "Save".  The Celebrity must be saved in "TemplateNPCs" folder. After saving, a window will appear asking if you’d like to visit The Sims Exchange to upload the Celebrity. Clicking “Yes” will take you to the exchange (if you have an active internet connection); clicking “Cancel” will return you to the main menu.  

Celebrity Manager
--------------

Instructions on how to swap a current Celebrity with a created Celebrity.
----------------------------------------------------------
* Note, you have to actually visit the neighborhood you'd like to place your Celebrity in before you can import them.
1. Click "Import" after Make-A-Celebrity loads.
2. Select the Neighborhood you'd like your Celebrity to appear in.
3. Select the Celebrity you'd like to replace.
4. Select the Celebrity you’ve made that you would like to see in Studio Town.
5. After this step you will be returned to the main menu.
