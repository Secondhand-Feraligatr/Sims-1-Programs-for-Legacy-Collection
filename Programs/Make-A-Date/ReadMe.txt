Overview
--------

Make-A-Date allows you to create your perfect match and take them out on the town.  Customize their looks, personality, interests and career!  Did you make a simply irresistible Sim that has everyone turning their heads?  Then upload 'em to the Date Exchange at thesims.ea.com and see how they stack up against the competition.
You must have The Sims Hot Date installed in order to use The Sims Make-A-Date.


Instructions on the use of The Sims Make-A-Date.
-----------------------------------------------
To make a date:
1. Click the "Create" button after Make-A-Date loads.
2. Select a gender, body type, skin type, body and head.  Click "Next".
3. Enter a name (less than 30 characters) and a description (1,024 characters or less) for your date.  Click "Next".
4. Give your date a personality.  You may use up to 25 personality points or click on the "Astrological Sign" box to    give your date a personality based on an astrological sign.  Click "Next".
5. Assign interests to your date.  You must provide the Top 5 Interests.  The Low Interests are optional.  Click       "Next".
6. Choose a job and job level for your date.  They must be employed.  Click "Next".
7. Click "Save Sim".  You may click "Back" to modify any of your date's traits.
8. Enter the name you wish to save the date as and click "Save".  The date must be saved in "TemplateNPCs" folder.



Instructions on how to swap a Townie with a created date.
----------------------------------------------------------
1. Click "Replace" after Make-A-Date loads.
2. Select the Neighborhood you'd like to move your date to.
3. Select the Townie you'd like to replace.
4. Select the date you'd like to move in.
5. Confirm change.
